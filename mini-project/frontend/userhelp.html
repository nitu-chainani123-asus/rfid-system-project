Frequently Asked Questions

How does the RFID attendance system work?
Students tap their RFID card on the reader and the system records the attendance automatically.
What if my attendance is missing?
You can submit an attendance correction request from this page or contact the administrator.
Who should I contact for technical issues?
You should contact the system administrator using the Contact Administrator section.
Can I download my attendance report?
Yes, you can download your attendance report from the Reports section in the dashboard.
